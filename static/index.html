⚙ HOW IT WORKS TF-IDF + Cosine Similarity, built from scratch
STEP 01
Tokenize
Lowercase text, remove punctuation, and tokenize into meaningful terms.
STEP 02
TF-IDF Vectors
Compute Term Frequency × Inverse Document Frequency to weight words by importance.
STEP 03
Cosine Similarity
Compare the query vector against every document vector. Rank by angle closeness.
